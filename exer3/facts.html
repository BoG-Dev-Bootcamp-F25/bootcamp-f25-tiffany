What are some fun facts about the Harry Potter series?

In Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix, Arthur Weasley was originally supposed to die, but his death was replaced with the death of Sirius.

Fred and George Weasley, perpetual jokers, were born on April Fools’ Day.

Makeup artists applied Harry’s scar over 2,000 times, and Radcliffe went through 160 pairs of glasses.
